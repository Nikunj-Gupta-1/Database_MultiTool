What is subdomain takeover? A subdomain points to a third-party service (via CNAME record) that no longer has a claim on that subdomain. An attacker can register on that service and serve malicious content under your domain. Candidates listed here have been confirmed by checking both the CNAME record and the fingerprint in the HTTP response.
